9 NCERT Curriculum To enable our students to compete with the best in the country, NCERT curriculum was implemented within a year, including training 30,000 teachers, translation of books into telugu, and printing of books. All books were delivered to schools before start of academic year. Accordingly, academic year has been changed to April-March and school timings has been changed to ensure that very young children don’t have to attend school very early in the morning. Also, school timings have been increased to provide more academic time and holiday calendar has been revised for more academic time.
